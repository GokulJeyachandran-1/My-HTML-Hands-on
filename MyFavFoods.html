These are a few of my FAVORITE FOODS...


BIRYANI

BIRYANI is a delight to eat and a challenge for chefs to prepare.
The deliciously complex blend of flavors, spices, and aromas in biryani have come to epitomize the zenith of Indian cuisine.



CAKES

CAKE is a flour confection made from flour, sugar, and other ingredients, and is usually baked.
Every occasion calls for a cake. A small cake has the power to light up the party and make things extra special.



POTATO CRISPS

A potato chip or crisp is a thin slice of potato that has been either deep fried, baked, or air fried until crunchy.
It is ranked as the best snack ever. Eating a bowl fill of Potato chips makes me refreshing!
